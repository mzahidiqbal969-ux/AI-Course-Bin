🚀 AI Adoption in Fortune 500 Companies (2020–2025)

Welcome to one of the most insightful and future-focused datasets on Artificial Intelligence adoption across top global companies 🌍.

This dataset captures how Fortune 500–like companies are leveraging AI from 2020 to 2025, including trends in ROI, maturity, and real-world use cases.

📊 What’s Inside?

🔹 6,000 records
🔹 1,000 companies
🔹 6 years of data (2020–2025)

🧠 Key Features:
Company & Industry – Explore AI trends across sectors
Country & Company Type – Global and organizational insights
Revenue & Employee Size – Business scale vs AI adoption
Uses AI (Yes/No) – Adoption tracking
Use Cases – Real-world AI applications
AI ROI (%) – Measure financial impact
AI Maturity Score (0–100) – Track AI growth stages
